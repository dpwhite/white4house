Property Taxes
Property taxes continue to rise, placing a larger and larger burden on home owners. When you buy a house, a majority of homeowners have their property taxes escrowed into their mortgage. As a homeowner you may even factor in an increase in the property tax as a function of your home's value. What you don't factor in is that your property tax automatically increase 8% year after year. No on should be taxed out of their home.
 
Public Education Funding
Last year the Legislature reduced state funds for public education funding by $1.8 billion and replaced it with local tax money. This transfers a bigger burden on school districts to increase taxes on homeowners in the community. This is in addition to the $5.4 billion dollars in cuts from the 2011 budget. The state needs to increase funding for public education to reduce the burden on local taxpayers.
 
End Voter Discrimination
Texas needs to repeal the discriminatory SB 5 voter id law. The law places an undue burden on poor and elderly voters to vote in elections by requiring that they produce photo identification before casting a ballot.
